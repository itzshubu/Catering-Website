About Us

Looking for a perfect food affair to complement your special occasion? Look no further! Billa Caterers provide a sumptuous food itinerary to fulfill your each and every catering need. Be it a large, mid or small sized event, our full-range of outdoor and indoor catering services cover complete food preparation and presentation for formal and informal gatherings.

From an array of food items to choose from, Billa Caterers bring to you the best from all cuisines to suit your event, tastes and pockets even. So while you leave your food worries to us, we let you enjoy those special moments in all the glory. So let the celebrations begin!!

Our Services

Billa Caterers serve you the best with the best to serve. From birthday parties, corporate seminars to weddings and related functions, we give you a range of food items to choose for your occasion.

Our Mission

Your happiness. We at Billa Caterers strive really hard and go that extra mile to bring on your guests that smiling and appeasing burp after indulging into the best of the dishes that we offer. A successful event is what we always aim for.

Our Values

While we work towards adding new items to our menu, authenticity is never compromised for novelty. As we are constantly trying to make our menu dynamic by adding international flavours, Billa Caterers are bound to their Indian roots by the legacy that has been created over 3 decades.

Quality Assurance

Established since 1980, Billa Caterers is a brand in the catering niche reputed not only for its mouth watering menu but also for the quality of its food which has only gotten better with each passing day..

Our team

Manned with a team of 1000 hard working professionals at your service, we make sure that all your needs are fulfilled with precision par excellence, which lets you enjoy your special moments without those bouts of worry.
